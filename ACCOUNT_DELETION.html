Riento Account Deletion

Operator: Pieti Siivola

You can request deletion of your Riento account and associated data in either of the following ways.

Option 1: Request deletion in the app

  1. Open Riento.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Sign in to your account.
  4. Open the Account deletion section.
  5. Tap Request account deletion.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

Email Siivola.Labs@proton.me from the email address associated with your account and include a request to delete your Riento account.

What data is deleted

When an account deletion request is completed, Riento is intended to delete the associated authentication account and user-owned private records used by the service, including account-linked venue signals, manual placements, private venue reports, private music reports, and related export/deletion request records handled by the current admin process.

What may be retained temporarily or separately

  • Completed account deletion request records may be retained privately for audit/compliance purposes for a limited period.
  • Backups, logs, or other legal/compliance records may be retained for a limited time where required.

Contact

If you have questions about account deletion, contact Siivola.Labs@proton.me.
