Riento Support

Last updated: 2026-07-11

Riento helps people discover active nightlife venues in Helsinki using venue-level crowd and music signals. If you need help with the app, contact support by email.

Contact

Email: Siivola.Labs@proton.me

Please include the issue, your device model, iOS or Android version, app version if visible, and the venue or screen where the problem happened.

Common Help Topics

Account access Apple or Google sign-in Location permission Venue data correction Now-playing reports Data export Account deletion

Venue Or Crowd Issues

If a venue is missing, closed, duplicated, has the wrong address, or the crowd label feels wrong, you can send a private venue report inside the app. Reports are reviewed before venue data or public signals are changed.

Privacy And Data Requests

Riento does not show individual user locations or exact public crowd counts. Signed-in users can use Profile controls to delete stored crowd signals, request a data export, or request account deletion.

For account deletion instructions, visit Account Deletion.

Safety

Venue information, crowd labels, and music signals are estimates. Venues may be closed, full, private, age-restricted, or otherwise unavailable regardless of what the app shows. Riento is not an emergency service.
